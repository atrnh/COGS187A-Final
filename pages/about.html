about us

The Visual Arts department at UCSD was founded in 1969 and continues to provide a rigorous arts education to UCSD students.

The Chair's Statement will give you an insight into the Visual Arts department's interdisciplinary approach and its collaborations with researchers working in nanotechnology, materials science, and large-scale structural engineering, exploring new forms of distributed cognition, materials fabrication, sensing, and computer visualization.

The Dean's Statement introduces the new Structural and Materials Engineering building and how it provides a new platform for learning and collaboration for the Visual Arts department

Check out the history page to browse through a visual history of the Visual Arts department from 1969 to 1996
